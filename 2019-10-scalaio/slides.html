* + powerful type system * +- structural types * fp is hard and not idiomatic * most popular typed fronted - the default * bindings still dont work * unsafe - types lie ++++

ReasonML

* ocaml base & ecosystem * mutability? ++++

Elm

* opinionated * redux model * separated from js * most popular typed fp * handicapped haskell (no typeclasses) * tooling * bus factor ++++
